Privacy Policy

Lula Rose Company — last updated July 2026

This page describes how Lula Rose Company's internal shop-management application ("this application") handles data. This application is a private business tool built and used exclusively by Lula Rose Company to manage its own Etsy shop and, where connected, its own Pinterest business account. It is not offered to the public, does not have separate user accounts for other people, and is not used to collect data about site visitors, Etsy buyers, or Pinterest followers.

What data this application accesses

  • Lula Rose Company's own Etsy shop data, via Etsy's Open API, connected through Etsy's own OAuth login: listing details (titles, descriptions, tags, prices, images), order/receipt totals (units sold and revenue — not buyer names, addresses, or payment details), and review counts.
  • Publicly available Etsy data about other, unrelated shops (competitor benchmarking) — only information Etsy already shows publicly on any shop's storefront page (listing titles, tags, prices, review counts, shop name and icon). No private or non-public data about any other shop is accessed.
  • Lula Rose Company's own Pinterest business account data, once connected via Pinterest's OAuth login, for the purpose of publishing and managing pins linked to Lula Rose Company's own products.

What this application does NOT do

  • It does not collect personal data from Etsy buyers or Pinterest followers.
  • It does not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties for advertising.
  • It is not a public-facing product — access is restricted to Lula Rose Company.
  • It does not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising trackers.

Third-party services this application uses

To do its job, this application sends relevant data to a small number of third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policy:

  • Etsy (Open API) — reading and writing Lula Rose Company's own shop/listing data.
  • Pinterest (API) — publishing pins to Lula Rose Company's own Pinterest account.
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — used to help draft listing titles and descriptions from Lula Rose Company's own product information.
  • Google Sheets (optional) — internal reporting export, via a private service account.
  • MailerSend — email delivery for internal shop-performance reminders.

Data storage and security

Data is stored in a private database accessible only to Lula Rose Company, protected by a password. It is not published, indexed, or made available to the public.
